About

Whitaker Realty

Founded in 2001 by Steve and Chad Whitaker, Whitaker Realty is Fort Lauderdale's premier boutique luxury real estate firm — specializing in the city's most prestigious waterfront neighborhoods.

With over $1.5 billion in career sales, we are the recognized leaders in Rio Vista, Las Olas Isles, and Harbor Beach. Our exclusive listings represent the finest properties available — many never appearing on the open market.

At Whitaker Realty, every client receives the personalized attention and deep market expertise that only a father-and-son team with two decades of local dominance can deliver.
